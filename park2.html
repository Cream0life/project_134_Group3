About Toronto Island Park

Toronto Island Park s made up of 15 individual islands of all shapes and sizes. Just a short ferry ride from the city of Toronto, and all connected by boardwalks and walking trails,
the Toronto Islands are the perfect place to spend a day with the family, someone special, or on your own.


Activites to do in the park:
  1. Riding bikes

    • Over 20 kilometers of paved pathways crisscross the island
    • Bicycles are welcome on the ferries with no additional fee
    • Rentals are available from the concession at Centre Island Beach

  2. Paddle the Canals

    • Interspersed between the 15 islands are placid canals and narrow waterways that are best explored at water level
    • By boats / kayaks / stand up paddleboard
    • Board and kayaksrentals s are available through Toronto Island SUP, located near the Algonquin bridge

  3. Lounge on the Beaches

    • Four Blue Flag beaches
    • Soft golden sands
    • Clear clean water
    • Fun for all ages

  4. Go Fishing
    • A wide variety of fish can be found : jumbo northern pike, catfish, and largemouth bass
    • kayak and canoes available for rent which will give better fishing opportunities.

Attraction in Toronto Island Park:
  1. Centreville Amusement Park

  2. William Meany Maze

    • Has 1,200 Black Cedars planted in a winding design
    • The William Meany Maze is a short walk from the Centre Island ferry terminal

  3. Far Enough Farm

    • Over 40 different species of farmyard animals and exotic birds
    • A fully functioning farm with real farmers going about their daily chores
